## 1️⃣ Project Overview
Understanding customer purchasing behavior is essential for businesses seeking to improve sales strategies and customer targeting. This project analyzes demographic and lifestyle factors such as income, age, marital status, education level, region, and commute distance to identify patterns that influence bike purchasing decisions. The final outcome is a dynamic Excel dashboard that visually summarizes these relationships, making it easy to identify key drivers of purchase behavior.

---

## 2️⃣ Objectives

- Understand relationships between customer characteristics and purchase decisions  
- Identify factors influencing bike purchases  
- Compare purchasing behavior across demographics  
- Analyze income patterns among buyers vs non-buyers  
- Present findings through an interactive dashboard
